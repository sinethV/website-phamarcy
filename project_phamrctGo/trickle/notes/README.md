# PharmaCare Website

A comprehensive pharmacy and healthcare website built with React, featuring online consultations, appointment scheduling, product catalog, and patient management.

## Features

- **Homepage**: Hero section with company information and key features
- **Product Catalog**: Comprehensive medicine and supplement listing with search and filtering
- **Shopping Cart**: Full cart functionality with localStorage persistence
- **Product Details**: Detailed product pages with ingredients, dosage, and warnings
- **Blog**: Health and wellness articles with category filtering
- **Contact**: Contact form for customer inquiries
- **Schedule**: Consultation booking system with multiple appointment types
- **Login/Dashboard**: User authentication and appointment management
- **Search**: Medicine and supplement search functionality across the site

## Pages Structure

- `index.html` - Homepage with hero section and features
- `products.html` - Product catalog with search and filtering
- `product-detail.html` - Individual product detail pages
- `cart.html` - Shopping cart with quantity management
- `blog.html` - Health blog with articles and categories
- `contact.html` - Contact form and company information
- `schedule.html` - Consultation scheduling form
- `login.html` - User login and registration
- `dashboard.html` - User dashboard with appointments and quick actions

## Key Components

- **Header**: Navigation with search functionality and cart counter
- **HeroSection**: Main banner with call-to-action buttons
- **FeaturesSection**: Company benefits and services
- **ProductCatalog**: Product listing with search, filtering, and add to cart
- **ProductDetail**: Detailed product view with tabs and purchase options
- **Cart**: Shopping cart management with quantity controls
- **BlogList**: Article listing with filtering
- **ContactForm**: Customer inquiry form
- **ScheduleForm**: Appointment booking form
- **LoginForm**: User authentication
- **Dashboard**: User portal with appointment management
- **Footer**: Site links and contact information

## Shopping Features

- Product search and category filtering
- Add to cart functionality with localStorage
- Cart quantity management and item removal
- Product detail pages with comprehensive information
- Price display with sale indicators
- Stock status tracking

- Primary Color: #10b981 (Green)
